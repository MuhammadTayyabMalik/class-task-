1. Almond

Almonds are one of the most popular and nutritious dry fruits in the world, loved for their crunchy texture, mild flavor, and impressive health benefits. They are packed with protein, fiber, healthy fats, vitamin E, magnesium, and antioxidants that help support overall health. Many people start their day with soaked almonds because they provide steady energy and help keep hunger away for longer periods. Almonds are also known for supporting healthy skin and hair due to their high vitamin E content, which helps protect cells from damage.
